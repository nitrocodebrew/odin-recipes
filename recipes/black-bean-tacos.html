Black Bean Tacos

Nutrition Highlights

A great source of plant-based protein and fiber, these tacos are filling, nutritious, and naturally vegetarian.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • ¼ cup red onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup salsa
  • 1 limes, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the black beans, chili powder, cumin, and garlic powder.
  3. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beans are heated through.
  4. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  5. Fill each tortilla with the seasoned black beans.
  6. Top with lettuce, tomato, red onion, and salsa.
  7. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing over the tacos.

Optional Toppings

  • Sliced avocado
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Greek yogurt or sour cream
  • Jalapeño slices
  • Shredded cheese
  • Corn kernels
