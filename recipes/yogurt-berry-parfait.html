Greek Yogurt Berry Parfait

Nutrition Highlights

High in protein, calcium, and antioxidants.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, whatever floats your boat)
  • ¼ cup granola
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Divide half the yogurt between two glasses or bowls.
  2. Add a layer of berries.
  3. Add remaining yogurt.
  4. Top with granola and a drizzle of honey, if desired.
  5. Serve immediately
