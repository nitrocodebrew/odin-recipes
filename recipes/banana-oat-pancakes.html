Banana Oat Pancakes

Nutrition Highlights

Naturally sweetened, high in protein, and a good source of fiber.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup rolled oat
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Cooking spray or small amount of oil

Instructions

  1. Add the banana, eggs, oats, cinnamon, and optionally, vanilla extract to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray.
  4. Pour small portions of batter onto the skillet.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles form on surface.
  6. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with fresh fruit or a drizzle of honey or agave.
